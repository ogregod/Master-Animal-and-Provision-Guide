# BALDUR'S GATE MARKET CATTLE
## Trade Hub Standard Beef

*Baldur's Gate receives cattle from across the region, processes them to standard specifications, and ships throughout Western Faerûn. These aren't a single breed - they're a market standard that various cattle must meet for trade certification.*

**— Thadius Del Irontet, Master Butcher**

## QUICK STATS

**Purpose:** Beef Production (Trade Standard)

**Market Position:** Reliable middle-market beef
**Standard:** 1,200-1,300 lbs, consistent quality

## MARKET STANDARD REQUIREMENTS

**To sell as "Baldur's Gate Market Cattle":**
- Live weight: 1,200-1,300 lbs
- Health certified by guild inspector
- Select grade minimum (no Standard)
- Consistent processing yields
- Trade guild documentation

**Sources:** Daggerford, Goldenfields, Trielta Hills (selected animals only)

### **Processing Time**

**Total Active Labor:** 14 hours average

**Timeline:**
- Initial processing: 2-3 hours (slaughter to hung)
- Aging: 21 days (standard)
- Primal breakdown: 2-3 hours
- Retail fabrication: 6-8 hours

**Labor Cost:** ~56 sp (14 hours × 4 sp/hour journeyman rate)
