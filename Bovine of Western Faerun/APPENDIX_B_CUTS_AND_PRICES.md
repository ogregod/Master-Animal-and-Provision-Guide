## HOW TO USE THIS APPENDIX

**Baseline:** All prices are for **Daggerford Common** (standard beef cattle)
**Adjustments:** Multiply by breed modifier for other cattle types

**Breed Price Modifiers:**
- **Cormyrean Noble Black:** ×2.5-3
- **Goldenfields Prime:** ×2
- **Baldur's Gate Market:** ×1.3-1.5
- **Daggerford Common:** ×1 (baseline)
- **Trielta Hills Range:** ×0.8
- **Dairy Cull Cows:** ×0.9-1.1 (depending on breed)
- **Working Cattle Cull:** ×0.6-0.8 (tougher)
- **Wild Game (Aurochs, Highland):** ×1.3-1.5

## PRESERVED MEATS (Processed)

**Smoking/Curing adds 50-100% to base meat price:**

| Product | Base Cut | Fresh Price | Preserved Price | Preservation Time |
|---------|----------|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Smoked Brisket** | Brisket | 40-50 sp | 60-80 sp | 7-10 days smoking |
| **Corned Beef** | Brisket/Round | 40 sp | 55-65 sp | 5-7 days curing |
| **Beef Jerky** | Lean cuts | 0.9 sp/lb | 1.8-2.2 sp/lb | 2-3 days drying |
| **Smoked Sausage** | Ground beef + trim | 0.8 sp/lb | 1.5-2 sp/lb | 3-5 days smoking |

**Why More Expensive:**
- Labor (smoking, curing, tending)
- Ingredients (salt, spices, wood)
- Weight loss (30-40% water loss)
- Extended shelf life (weeks/months vs days)

## REGIONAL PRICE VARIATIONS

**Multiply baseline prices by regional modifier:**

| Region | Modifier | Reason |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Western Heartlands (Daggerford, Goldenfields)** | ×0.9 | Origin region, direct from farms |
| **Waterdeep** | ×1.0 | Baseline (major trade hub) |
| **Baldur's Gate** | ×1.0 | Baseline (major trade hub) |
| **Neverwinter** | ×1.1 | Northern supply chain |
| **Luskan** | ×1.15 | Far north, limited supply |
| **Icewind Dale (Ten-Towns)** | ×1.3-1.5 | Extreme isolation |
| **Amn/Tethyr** | ×1.1 | Southern import costs |
| **Calimshan** | ×1.5-2 | Desert scarcity, heat challenges |
| **Frontier Settlements** | ×1.3-1.6 | Transportation costs, scarcity |

**Example:**
- Ribeye in Daggerford: 4 sp (×0.9) = 3.6 sp
- Same ribeye in Luskan: 4 sp (×1.15) = 4.6 sp
- Same ribeye in Ten-Towns: 4 sp (×1.4) = 5.6 sp

| Purchase | Price | Savings vs Retail | Best For |

| **Whole Animal (live)** | 3.5 sp/lb (~440 sp for 1,250 lb steer) | ~200 sp savings | Established bases, long campaigns |

**Requirements:**
- Processing knowledge (Survival DC 12-15)
- Preservation capability (smoking, salting, or magical cold)
