The Chultan sun bear is **the alchemist's dream and the merchant's nightmare**. That tiny gallbladder - maybe 2 ounces - is worth 20 to 40 gold pieces. ## PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

### Size & Weight (**SMALLEST BEAR**)

**Adult Male:**
- **Live Weight:** 80-150 lbs (average 115 lbs) - **62% SMALLER than black bear**
- **Length:** 3.5-4.5 feet (including tail)
- **Shoulder Height:** 2-2.5 feet
- **Distinctive Feature:** Orange/yellow chest crescent marking (U-shaped, sun symbol)

**Adult Female:**
- **Live Weight:** 60-100 lbs (average 80 lbs)
- **Length:** 3-4 feet
- **Shoulder Height:** 1.5-2 feet

**Physical Features:**
- **Coat:** Black, short, sleek (tropical adaptation, not thick like northern bears)
- **Chest Mark:** Bright orange/yellow U-shaped crescent (distinctive, unique to sun bears)
- **Build:** Compact, small (tree-climbing specialist)
- **Claws:** Long, curved (2-3 inches) - climbing/digging termite mounds
- **Tongue:** Extremely long (8-10 inches) - honey/insect extraction
- **Ears:** Small, rounded
- **Muzzle:** Short, light-colored

**Compared to Black Bear:**
- 62% smaller (115 vs 300 lbs)
- Tropical (vs temperate)
- Arboreal (tree-dwelling vs ground)
- Insectivore diet (termites, honey vs berries, fish)
- Distinctive marking (orange crescent vs plain)

**Armor Class:** 11 (natural armor)

**Abilities:**

**Keen Smell:** Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell

**Behavior:**

**Hunting Difficulty:** **VERY EASY** (if you can find one)

## IMPORT ECONOMICS (DON'T DO IT)

### Why Importing Sun Bears Loses Money

**Cost to Import (Port Nyanzaru → Waterdeep):**
1. **Purchase in Chult:** 50-80 gp (live bear, local market)
2. **Ship Transport:** 200-300 gp (exotic animal cargo, 2-4 months voyage)
   - Specialized cage/crate: 20-30 gp
   - Food during voyage: 10-20 gp
   - Handler fees: 30-50 gp
   - Mortality risk: 20-30% (tropical animal, cold northern climate, stress)
3. **Total Import Cost:** 250-380 gp

**Revenue if Processed:**
- Gallbladder: 20-40 gp (best case)
- Meat/pelt/other: 15-25 gp
- **Total Revenue:** 35-65 gp

**Net Result:** **LOSS of 185-345 gp** (73-91% loss)

**Why Do Some Wizards Import Them?**
- NOT for butchering (they keep them alive)
- Research (study exotic creatures)
- Alchemical component farming (harvest bile from living bear - questionable ethics)
- Prestige (exotic menagerie status symbol)

**Verdict:** NEVER import sun bears for butchering. Only process if found locally.
