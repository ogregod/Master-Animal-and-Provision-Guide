## CONSISTENCY NOTES FOR LEAFY GREENS EXPANSION

**Pricing Structure:**
- Basic lettuces: 1-3 cp per head (iceberg cheapest, specialty types more expensive)
- Specialty greens: 2-6 cp per quantity (baby greens most expensive)
- Wild/foraged greens: 1-4 cp per quantity (abundant wilds cheapest, seasonal specialties more)
- Cooking greens: 1-4 cp per substantial quantity (hardy greens most affordable)

**Weight/Uses Calculations:**
- Tender salad greens: 3-6 uses per small quantity (2-3 oz servings)
- Hardy cooking greens: 4-10 uses per larger quantity (3-5 oz servings)
- Specialty/delicate items: 3-6 uses per small quantity (1-2 oz servings)
- Wild foraged: 4-6 uses per quantity (variable sizes, seasonal pricing)

**Storage Life Patterns:**
- Delicate salad greens: 1-4 days (butterhead, baby greens most perishable)
- Hardy lettuces: 1-2 weeks (iceberg, endive best storage)
- Cooking greens: 1-2 weeks (collards, chard hardiest)
- Wild greens: 1-7 days (depends on type and harvesting conditions)

**Seasonal Availability:**
- Year-round: Basic lettuces, common cooking greens
- Spring specialty: Wild foraged greens, spring tonics
- Cool weather: Endive, hardier varieties
- Warm weather: Tender varieties, baby greens

**Regional Specialties:**
- Used established growing regions (Trielta Hills, Silverymoon, Daggerford, etc.)
- Connected climate to logical growing conditions
- Wild varieties available throughout region
- Import/cultivation economics maintained
