### BONE STOCK

**Normal Wolf:**
- Bones used: 97 lbs (femurs, knuckles, vertebrae, ribs)
- Water: 18 gallons
- Simmer time: 24 hours
- Yield: 13 gallons = 104 pints
- **Price:** 3.5 sp per pint
- **Revenue:** 36.4 gp

**Alpha Wolf:**
- Bones used: 630.5 lbs
- Water: 117 gallons
- Yield: 780 pints (15% bonus due to larger marrow cavities)
- **Price:** 3.5 sp per pint
- **Revenue:** 273 gp

**Stock Aromatics (per batch):**
| Ingredient | Amount | Source | Cost |
|------------|--------|--------|------|
| Yellow Onions | 1 onion | Sword Coast Yellow Onions (2 cp/bag) | 1 cp |
| Carrots | 2 carrots | Silverymoon Carrots (5 cp/bunch) | 3 cp |
| Celery | 2 ribs | Celery (3 cp/bundle) | 1 cp |
| Bay Leaves | 2 leaves | Bay Leaves (3 sp/pkg) | 4 cp |
| Black Peppercorns | 10 whole | Black Peppercorns (1 sp/pkg) | ~1 cp |
| **TOTAL** | | | **~10 cp per batch** |

**Stock Making Process:**
1. Roast bones at 425°F for 45 minutes (develops flavor)
2. Cover with cold water in large pot
3. Add aromatics
4. Bring to gentle simmer (NOT boiling - makes it cloudy)
5. Skim scum for first 30 minutes
6. Simmer 24 hours
7. Strain through cheesecloth
8. Reduce by half for concentrated stock

**Storage:**
- Fresh: 1 week refrigerated
- Frozen: 6 months in jars
- Dried (bouillon): 1 year

### RENDERED TALLOW

**Normal Wolf:**
- Raw fat collected: 39 lbs
- Rendering loss (10%): 4 lbs
- **Yield: 35 lbs** clean tallow
- **Price:** 2 sp/lb (bulk)
- **Revenue:** 7 gp

**Alpha Wolf:**
- Raw fat collected: 254 lbs
- Rendering loss (10%): 25 lbs
- **Yield: 229 lbs** clean tallow
- **Price:** 2 sp/lb (bulk)
- **Revenue:** 45.8 gp

**Rendering Process:**
1. Cut fat into 1-inch cubes
2. Place in large pot on low heat (200-250°F)
3. Let melt slowly for 6-8 hours, stirring occasionally
4. Strain through cloth into jars while hot
5. Cool to solidify (creamy white when solid, golden when liquid)

**Uses:**
- Cooking fat (smoke point 420°F)
- Soap making
- Leather conditioning
- Candle making
- Emergency lamp fuel

## Critical Warnings

⚠️ **Wolf meat is GAMEY** - Requires proper seasoning and cooking
⚠️ **Do NOT overcook lean cuts** - Makes them tough as leather
⚠️ **Use 60/40 fat ratio for sausage** - Per General Butcher Guide standard for gamey meat
⚠️ **Blink Node is FRAGILE** - Rupture = loss of 100 gp (normal) or 800 gp (Alpha)
⚠️ **Frost Sac is VOLATILE** - Handle carefully, keep cold
⚠️ **Always use guild rates if you're a member** - Better prices, access to facilities
