## üí≥ STEP 4: Processing Fees & Timeline

*"Alright, so here's what this is gonna cost you, and when you can pick it up."*

### **PROCESSING FEES**

**Guild Butcher (Recommended):**
- Base processing: **50 gp** (covers labor, materials, facility use)
- Guild commission: **5% of total value** (quality guarantee)
  - *"You get guild certification. Means the meat is properly handled, everything's up to code."*

**Independent Butcher (Cheaper but riskier):**
- Base processing: **35-40 gp** (no guild oversight)
- No commission, but no guarantee of quality
  - *"Look, I'm good at what I do, but if something goes wrong, you're on your own."*

**Additional Fees:**
- Bacon curing: **+1 gp** (salt & wood)
- Long-term storage: **5 gp per week** (if you need me to hold it)
- Rush processing: **+25 gp** (if you need it done in 1 day instead of 2-3)
