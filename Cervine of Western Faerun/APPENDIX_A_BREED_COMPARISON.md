5. **Special properties** (Moonlit Deer = alchemical value overrides size)

## II. PROCESSING YIELDS AND ECONOMICS

### **LIVE TO RETAIL YIELD PROGRESSION**

**ALL BREEDS: 65% DRESSING × 45% RETAIL = 29% TOTAL YIELD**

| Breed | Live Weight | Dressed (65%) | Retail (45%) | Live-to-Retail (29%) |
|-------|-------------|---------------|--------------|----------------------|
| **Giant Elk** | 2,000 lbs | 1,300 lbs | 585 lbs | 580 lbs (29%) |
| **Evermoor Moose** | 1,200 lbs | 780 lbs | 351 lbs | 348 lbs (29%) |
| **High Moor Wapiti** | 750 lbs | 488 lbs | 220 lbs | 217.5 lbs (29%) |
| **Moonshae Island Elk** | 600 lbs | 390 lbs | 176 lbs | 174 lbs (29%) |
| **Icewind Dale Caribou** | 365 lbs | 237 lbs | 107 lbs | 106 lbs (29%) |
| **Trielta Red Deer** | 325 lbs | 211 lbs | 95 lbs | 94 lbs (29%) |
| **High Forest Mule Deer** | 200 lbs | 130 lbs | 59 lbs | 58 lbs (29%) |
| **Sword Coast Whitetail** | 180 lbs | 117 lbs | 53 lbs | 52 lbs (29%) |
| **Neverwinter Fallow** | 160 lbs | 104 lbs | 47 lbs | 46 lbs (29%) |
| **Feywild Moonlit Deer** | 125 lbs | 81 lbs | 37 lbs | 36 lbs (29%) |
| **Chultan Pygmy Antelope** | 70 lbs | 46 lbs | 21 lbs | 20 lbs (29%) |
| **Ardeep Roe Deer** | 60 lbs | 39 lbs | 18 lbs | 17 lbs (29%) |

**YIELD CONSISTENCY:**
- **Formula is UNIVERSAL:** Works for 60 lb Roe Deer to 2,000 lb Giant Elk
- **65% dressing:** Accounts for hide, head, legs, organs, blood (35% loss)
- **45% retail:** Accounts for bones, trim, fat, processing loss (55% of dressed = 45% loss)
- **29% final yield:** Nearly 1/3 of live weight becomes retail cuts

**SCALING EXAMPLE:**
- **1 Giant Elk (2,000 lbs) = 11.1 × Whitetail (180 lbs) in live weight**
- **Giant Elk retail (580 lbs) = 11.1 × Whitetail retail (52 lbs)**
- **Perfect linear scaling** (formula holds across all sizes)

## VI. TROPHY SCORING SYSTEMS

### **ANTLER SCORING STANDARDS (GUILD OF HUNTERS)**

**SCORING COMPONENTS (All Branching/Palmated Antlers)**

**Points System (out of 400 total):**

**1. SPREAD (40% = 160 points max):**
- Widest outside measurement (inch)
- Measured perpendicular to centerline of skull
- **Formula:** Spread (inches) × 1.6 = Points
- **Example:** 100-inch spread = 160 points

**2. MAIN BEAM LENGTH (25% = 100 points max):**
- Longest continuous measurement along main beam
- Measured from base to furthest point
- Average both sides (if unequal)
- **Formula:** Beam length (inches) × 1.25 = Points
- **Example:** 60-inch beam = 75 points

**3. TOTAL POINTS (20% = 80 points max):**
- Count all points >1 inch length
- Both sides combined
- **Formula:** Total points × 2 = Points
- **Example:** 30 points = 60 points

**4. MASS/CIRCUMFERENCE (10% = 40 points max):**
- Measure circumference at 4 locations per beam
- Average measurements
- **Formula:** Average circumference (inches) × 5 = Points
- **Example:** 8-inch circumference = 40 points

**5. SYMMETRY (5% = 20 points max):**
- Compare left and right antlers
- Deduct for asymmetry, award for perfect match
- **Formula:** Subjective (scorer judgment)
- Perfect symmetry: +20 points
- Minor asymmetry: +5-15 points
- Major asymmetry: 0-5 points

**TOTAL SCORE:** Sum of all components (max 400 points)

**CARIBOU: PRESERVATION SPECIALIST**
- **70-80% of caribou meat is preserved** (smoked/salted, not sold fresh)
- **Why?** Volume harvest (5-10 caribou per season), migration timing, Ten-Towns winter storage
- **Methods:** Smoke-drying (most common), salt-curing, pemmican (traditional Reghed)

**FRESH VENISON SHELF LIFE:**
- Temperature: 34-38°F (cooler storage)
- **Whole quarters:** 10-14 days
- **Retail cuts:** 5-8 days
- **Ground venison:** 2-4 days (higher surface area = faster spoilage)

**SMOKING:**
- Wood types: Hickory (strong), applewood (sweet), cherry (mild), oak (traditional)
- Smoke time: 12-24 hours (hot smoke), 2-5 days (cold smoke)
- **Best cuts:** Roasts, whole muscles (backstrap, ham), sausages

**SALTING:**
- Salt ratio: 8-12% salt by weight
- Cure time: 7-14 days (depending on thickness)
- **Best cuts:** Whole muscles, boneless cuts (salt penetrates evenly)

**FREEZING:**
- Natural (winter): Hang outside in Nightal-Alturiak (below 32°F)
- Magical (Wall of Ice, Create Food/Water): Expensive, rare
- **Best cuts:** All cuts (preserves quality well)
