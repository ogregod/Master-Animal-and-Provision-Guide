## CRITICAL EQUIPMENT PRINCIPLES

### **Sharp Tools Save Lives**
This isn't metaphor. More butchers are injured by dull knives than sharp ones. Dull blades slip, require excessive pressure, and make ragged cuts that heal poorly. Sharp blades go exactly where you direct them.

### **Proper Tool for the Task**
A boning knife excels at working around joints but fails at breaking down large sections. A cleaver splits bones efficiently but destroys delicate work. Learn which tool serves which purpose.

### **Maintenance Is Not Optional**
A knife that's sharp Monday and dull Wednesday isn't properly maintained. Daily maintenance takes minutes. Correcting neglect takes hours or replacement costs.

### **Quality Pays for Itself**
Professional tools cost 5-10 times more than cheap ones but last 20-30 times longer while performing better daily. The mathematics favor quality.

### **Clean Tools Prevent Disease**
Cross-contamination kills customers. Clean tools, clean surfaces, clean hands, clean water. Make sanitation automatic.

**What you're buying:** Heavy-duty, high-carbon steel blade designed for general butchering work.
