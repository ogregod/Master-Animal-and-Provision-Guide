## HOW TO USE THIS APPENDIX

**Baseline:** All prices are for **Daggerford Farm Pig** (standard all-purpose pork)
**Adjustments:** Multiply by breed modifier for other pig types

**Breed Price Modifiers:**
- **Cormyrean Black:** ×1.8-2.2 (ultra-premium, acorn-fed)
- **Goldenfields Heritage:** ×1.5-1.8 (grain-fed premium)
- **Trielta Forest:** ×1.3-1.5 (acorn-finished country)
- **Neverwinter Northern:** ×1.1-1.3 (cold-climate, lard specialist)
- **Daggerford Farm:** ×1 (baseline)
- **Waterdeep Common:** ×0.9-1 (urban commercial)
- **Calishite Bristleback:** ×1.4-1.7 (exotic, scarcity premium)
- **Wild Boar:** ×1.2-1.4 (gamey, trophy value separate)

## SEASONAL PRICING MODIFIERS

**SPRING (March-May):**
- Fresh pork: +10-15% (limited supply)
- Preserved products: +20-25% (winter stock depleting)

**SUMMER (June-August):**
- Fresh pork: +5-10% (moderate supply)
- Preserved products: +10-15% (spring/summer stock)

**FALL (September-November) - BEST PRICES:**
- Fresh pork: Baseline to -5% (PEAK SUPPLY, slaughter season)
- **Best buying time for bulk**

**WINTER (December-February):**
- Fresh pork: +15-25% (limited, frozen only)
- Preserved products: Peak demand (bacon, ham, sausage)

**Player asks: "What's the most expensive pork?"**
- Cormyrean 18-month aged ham: 20-25 sp/lb
